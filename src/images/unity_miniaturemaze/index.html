Miniature Maze

A Unity and C# assignment with theme “miniature maze”. The idea was to interact with events within the miniature world while trying to find your way out. I tried to recreate the creepy atmosphere of a traditional Unity game. The addition of LEGO pieces gives the illusion that the player is indeed inside a miniature world. The main character is a sleepwalker that tries to make his way to his bed, but little creepy zombies are trying to keep him wake or even kill him. Of course he can fight back and kill them first gaining points in the process. To make the gameplay more interesting I added the glowing LEGOs that lett the player collect even more points. When the player has collected all the legos then he can finally enter his bedroom and sleep.
